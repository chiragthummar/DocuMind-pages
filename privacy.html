Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Your privacy matters. DocuMind is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your documents stay on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

DocuMind collects minimal information necessary to provide you with the best experience:

  • Documents & Scans: All PDFs, scanned documents, and images are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers.
  • API Keys: If you provide API keys for AI features (Claude, OpenAI), they are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device and are never transmitted to us.
  • App Preferences: Settings like theme, language, and export preferences are stored locally using UserDefaults.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

2. Third-Party AI Services & Data Sharing

Important: DocuMind's AI features send your document text to third-party AI providers. You must explicitly consent to this data sharing before using any AI feature for the first time.

What data is sent:

  • Document text content that you choose to analyze (e.g., when you tap Summarize, Translate, or Extract)
  • Your typed prompts and questions when using AI Chat
  • Selected text for translation, summarization, or data extraction

Who receives your data:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — if you configure Claude as your AI provider. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI (GPT API) — if you configure OpenAI as your AI provider. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

How data sharing works:

  • Data is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you configure — DocuMind does not act as an intermediary.
  • You provide your own API key, which is stored locally in the iOS Keychain.
  • No data is sent automatically — AI features are activated only by your explicit action (tapping an AI tool button).
  • DocuMind does not store, log, or collect any data sent to or received from AI providers.

Your consent:

  • Before using any AI feature for the first time, DocuMind displays a data disclosure and consent screen explaining what data is sent and to whom.
  • You must tap "I Understand & Agree" to proceed. If you decline, no data is sent and AI features remain unavailable.
  • You can revoke consent at any time by removing your API key in Settings or resetting app settings.
  • All PDF tools (scan, edit, sign, merge, watermark, etc.) work fully offline without any data sharing.

3. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync (Pro feature), your documents are synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. This data is encrypted and managed by Apple under their privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. OCR & Document Processing

All OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and document processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No document content is sent to external servers for processing.

5. Analytics & Tracking

DocuMind does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage patterns, browsing habits, or document content.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Period.

7. Data Security

  • API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption.
  • Documents are stored in the app's sandboxed container, protected by iOS security.
  • Password-protected PDFs use industry-standard encryption.

8. Children's Privacy

DocuMind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete: Remove any document at any time from within the app.
  • Export: Export your documents in multiple formats at any time.
  • Reset: Use "Reset All Settings" to clear all preferences.
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
